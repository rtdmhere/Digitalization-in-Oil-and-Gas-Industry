# Digitalization in Petroleum Engineering: Transforming the Industry

## Overview
This paper presentation explores how digital technologies are transforming Oil and Gas industry and oilfield operations.

## Topics Covered
- Artificial Intelligence (AI)
- Internet of Things (IoT)
- Big Data Analytics
- Cloud Computing
- Digital Twins
- Cybersecurity
- Sustainability in Oil & Gas

## Key Objectives
- Improve operational efficiency
- Reduce environmental impact
- Enhance data-driven decision making
- Promote sustainable petroleum operations
